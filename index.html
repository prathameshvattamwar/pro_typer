Typing Tips

  • Posture: Sit up straight, feet flat on the floor. Keep wrists straight, not resting on the desk.
  • Home Row: Always return your fingers to the home row keys (ASDF JKL;). Use the bumps on F and J keys to find them without looking.
  • Finger Placement: Assign specific fingers to specific keys and stick to it. (Visual keyboard guide helps!)
  • Accuracy over Speed: Focus on typing correctly first. Speed will come naturally with practice.
  • Don't Look Down: Train yourself to keep your eyes on the screen, not your hands.
  • Rhythm: Try to maintain a steady typing rhythm rather than bursts of speed.
  • Practice Regularly: Consistent, short practice sessions are more effective than infrequent long ones.
  • Take Breaks: Avoid strain by taking short breaks during long typing sessions.
